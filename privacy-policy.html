Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

1. Overview and Commitment

At The Roofing Advisor (accessible via TheRoofingAdvisor.com), we take the security of your dynamic personal identifiers and contact parameters extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy details the exact pathways concerning information collection, distribution structures, and client consent procedures applied across our regional databases and interactive estimating tools.

By utilizing our interactive Roofing Cost Calculator or completing our free Contractor Bid Connector Form, you accept the data handling frameworks described herein.

2. Information We Collect

We gather basic personal identifiers and structural parameters to successfully provide localized roofing price indexes and coordinate quotes with pre-screened partner builders:

  • Contact Parameter Identifiers: Your first and last name, cellular phone number, and physical physical/electronic mailing addresses.
  • Zoning/Geographical Data: Your 5-digit home postal zip code (used fundamentally to calculate regional cost multipliers and match local contractor networks).
  • Structural Project Constraints: Estimates of your target roofing footprint size (square footage), desired shingle/metal materials, pitches gradients, layers, and tear-off parameters selected during computational simulator processes.
  • Technical Metadata: Non-personally identifiable indicators like dynamic IP addresses, browser agents, and tracking cookie indices used strictly to monitor site metrics.

3. How Information is Disclosed and Used

The Roofing Advisor utilizes accumulated data structures exclusively to fulfill user-requested goals:

  • Localized Estimates: Running mathematical calculations of your structural variables to display localized material and labor cost distributions.
  • Contractor Bidding Referrals: When you opt to submit parameters for private quotes, we safely transmit structural details and contact identifiers to up to three (3) pre-screened, fully licensed, and bonded local roofing contractors in your territory region.
  • Administrative Logs: Storing inquiries in localized browser storage logs to ensure support staff can successfully resolve support tickets.

We do NOT sell, lease, or distribute your email or phone number to robocall catalogs or automated mass marketing matrices. Your parameters are only communicated directly to specific verified contracting entities that represent bids in your area.

4. Safety and Encrypted Handshake

All data transmitted through our online calculation forms and lead generators employs industry-standard 256-Bit SSL Handshake socket layers to block intercept pathways. While we run state-of-the-art protections, no transmission over the digital web is 100% impenetrable; as such, users offer identifiers at their own discretion.

5. Cookies and Web Telemetry

We leverage browser cookie parameters strictly to save localized values on our interactive sliders (such as pre-filled zip code targets), keeping experiences seamless when navigating between guides. You have complete permission to block cookies in browser preferences, though doing so might disable certain responsive memory features on our interactive tools.

6. Data Control and Suppression Rights

Under federal and state statutes, homeowners retain absolute sovereignty over their data profiles. If you reside in California (CCPA) or other states with dynamic data privacy bills:

You may contact us at any time to request:

  • Detailed disclosure of collected parameters.
  • Complete removal or permanent suppression of contact identifiers in our databases.
  • Exclusion of parameters from matched regional third-party contractor networks.

To invoke your suppression rights, please submit a designated query through our Contact Us Portal, select the subject line "Privacy Concern / Data Removal", and we will process the suppression request within 24 working hours.

7. Adjustments to This Document

The Roofing Advisor reserves direct authorization to adjust these Privacy Policy terms periodically as regional builder regulations shift. All updates become instantly enforceable upon being published on this page.

For further queries, email us directly at contact@theroofingadvisor.com.
